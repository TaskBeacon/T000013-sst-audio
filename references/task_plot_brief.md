Title: Stop-Signal Task (SST-Audio)
Construct: response inhibition / auditory stop signal

Purpose:
Show the participant-visible auditory stop-signal timeline with adaptive SSD.

Rows / conditions:
- Go trial: white left/right arrow; press F for left or J for right within the go window.
- Stop trial: white arrow appears first, then an auditory beep plays after SSD; withhold response.

Timeline:
- Fixation: random 800-1000 ms; no scored response; centered +.
- Go response window: 1000 ms; press F/J according to arrow direction; white arrow.
- Timeout feedback: 800 ms; shown only after go timeout.
- Pre-stop go window: adaptive SSD 50-500 ms; early response is stop failure; white arrow.
- Stop-signal window: remaining go window after SSD; auditory beep stop signal; white arrow remains; withhold response.

Notes:
- Left arrow = F; right arrow = J.
- SSD starts at 250 ms and adapts by 50 ms between 50-500 ms.
- Staircase targets about 50% stop success.
